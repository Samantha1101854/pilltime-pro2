About PillTime Pro

Supporting Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health & Well-Being

Our Mission

PillTime Pro is dedicated to improving medication adherence and promoting better health outcomes through intelligent reminder systems and comprehensive tracking tools.

We believe that proper medication management is essential for achieving Sustainable Development Goal 3: Ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages.

Privacy Focused

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-Being

SDG 3

Target 3.8: Achieve universal health coverage, including access to quality essential health-care services and access to safe, effective, quality and affordable essential medicines and vaccines for all.

PillTime Pro contributes to this goal by:

  • Improving medication adherence through reliable reminders
  • Providing accessible medication management tools
  • Supporting better health outcomes through consistent tracking
  • Empowering individuals to take control of their health

Privacy & Security

PillTime Pro operates entirely in your browser. All your medication data, reminders, and history are stored locally on your device using localStorage.

No data is sent to any servers. Your health information remains private and secure on your own device.
